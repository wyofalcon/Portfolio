The DIRTEE Methodology

Design In Real Time, Early Engagement

DIRTEE is my signature approach to building systems, workflows, and prototypes. It collapses the gap between idea and execution, while ensuring stakeholders are involved from the very beginning. By designing in real time and engaging early, I create solutions that are technically sound, trusted, and aligned with real needs.

Core Principles

  • Design in Real Time: Move from concept to prototype quickly, using modular builds and rapid iteration.
  • Early Engagement: Involve stakeholders and users at the start, building trust and catching misalignments early.

Why DIRTEE Matters

  • Speed with Substance: Accelerates innovation without sacrificing rigor.
  • Trust by Design: Early engagement ensures solutions are ethical, usable, and embraced.
  • Cross-Disciplinary Fit: Works equally well for software, hardware, and workflow design.
  • Evidence of Mastery: Demonstrates adaptability, systems thinking, and collaborative leadership.
